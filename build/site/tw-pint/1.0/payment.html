TW-PINT Candidate Specification

TW-PINT inherits payment means structures from PINT and UBL 2.1.

Payment details may be represented through cac:PaymentMeans, cbc:PaymentMeansCode, and cac:PayeeFinancialAccount.

Taiwan-specific payment extensions are reserved for future versions.
