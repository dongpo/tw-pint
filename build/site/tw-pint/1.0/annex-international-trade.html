Annex C. International Trade Implementation Scenarios

Taiwan exporter to Singapore buyer

A Taiwan exporter issues a TW-PINT invoice. The buyer receives it through the Peppol network and processes it according to local Singapore requirements.

Taiwan exporter to Japan buyer

A Taiwan exporter issues a TW-PINT invoice to a Japanese buyer. The document should remain interoperable with JP-PINT receiving practices where possible.

Foreign supplier to Taiwan buyer

A Taiwan buyer receives an invoice from a foreign PINT jurisdiction. The invoice is mapped to Taiwan accounting and tax reporting requirements.

Cross-border credit note handling

Credit notes should use standard PINT preceding invoice references and preserve traceability to the original invoice.
