TW-PINT Candidate Specification

Taiwan Jurisdiction Extension Candidate of Peppol International Invoice Transaction (PINT).

This is a candidate specification prepared for community review. It is not an official OpenPeppol publication and has not been approved by OpenPeppol or a Taiwan Peppol Authority.

TW-PINT uses Peppol PINT as the international invoice exchange layer and Taiwan eGUI as the domestic tax reporting layer. TW-PINT acts as the interoperability bridge between the two.
