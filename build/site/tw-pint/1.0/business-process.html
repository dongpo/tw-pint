TW-PINT Candidate Specification

TW-PINT follows the PINT billing process for issuing an invoice from a seller to a buyer through Peppol-compatible exchange.

  1. Seller creates a UBL 2.1 Invoice.

  2. Sender validates the document against UBL, PINT shared rules, and TW-PINT rules.

  3. Sender Access Point transmits the document through the Peppol network.

  4. Receiver validates and processes the invoice.

  5. If required, a local gateway maps the invoice to Taiwan eGUI-related processes.
