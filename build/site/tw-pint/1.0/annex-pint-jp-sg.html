TW-PINT Candidate Specification

TW-PINT inherits the PINT core invoice model, including seller, buyer, invoice line, monetary total, tax total, payment means and document references.

TW-PINT aligns PINT with Taiwan Business Tax by profiling tax categories and rates. Distinct Taiwan content includes UBN usage, Taiwan invoice number considerations and eGUI bridge guidance.

TW-PINT should avoid unnecessary divergence so that Taiwan systems can exchange invoices with JP-PINT, SG-PINT and other PINT jurisdictions.
