TW-PINT Candidate Specification

In scope

  • UBL 2.1 Invoice

  • UBL 2.1 CreditNote as a related correction document

  • B2B domestic invoice exchange in Taiwan

  • B2B export invoice exchange from Taiwan to foreign buyers

  • inbound invoice exchange from foreign sellers to Taiwan buyers

  • Taiwan Business Tax mainstream cases

  • standard-rated, zero-rated, and exempt tax categories

  • Taiwan Unified Business Number (UBN)

  • Peppol endpoint identification

  • eGUI gateway mapping considerations

Out of scope

  • B2C carrier handling

  • donation code and lottery handling

  • real-time tax clearance

  • complete replacement of Taiwan eGUI MIG specifications

  • consumer-facing eGUI functions

  • sector-specific special tax regimes

  • legal determination of tax liability

Boundary principle

TW-PINT SHALL NOT redefine the Taiwan eGUI legal reporting system. TW-PINT defines how invoice information may be exchanged using Peppol and how such information may be bridged into Taiwan eGUI-related processes by local service providers.
