5. Technical Details

5.1. BIS Identifiers

The TW-PINT CustomizationID is a candidate value and must be finalized through governance and community review.

5.2. Datatypes

TW-PINT inherits PINT semantic datatypes and adds Taiwan-specific identifier usage.

5.3. UBL schemas and namespaces

TW-PINT uses OASIS UBL 2.1 Invoice and CreditNote syntax.

5.4. Glossary

The glossary should include PINT, TW-PINT, eGUI, UBN, Business Tax, Access Point, SMP, SML and Peppol Authority.

5.5. TW-PINT specialization architecture

TW-PINT inherits shared content from PINT, adds Taiwan aligned restrictions for tax and identifiers, and defines Taiwan distinct content such as UBN usage and eGUI bridge mapping guidance.
