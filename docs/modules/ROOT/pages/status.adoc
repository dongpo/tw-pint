= Statement of Status

This specification is a candidate version of TW-PINT prepared for public and community review.

It is intended to support discussion among Taiwan government agencies, prospective Taiwan Peppol Authority stakeholders, Access Point providers, ERP and e-invoicing service providers, Taiwan eGUI service providers, international trading partners, and the OpenPeppol community.

This document is not an official legal, tax, or Peppol conformance source.
