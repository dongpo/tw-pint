= Business Process

TW-PINT follows the PINT billing process for issuing an invoice from a seller to a buyer through Peppol-compatible exchange.

. Seller creates a UBL 2.1 Invoice.
. Sender validates the document against UBL, PINT shared rules, and TW-PINT rules.
. Sender Access Point transmits the document through the Peppol network.
. Receiver validates and processes the invoice.
. If required, a local gateway maps the invoice to Taiwan eGUI-related processes.
