== Architectural distinction

[cols="1,2,2", options="header"]
|===
|Layer |Peppol / TW-PINT |Taiwan eGUI

|Primary role |Business document exchange |Domestic tax reporting and invoice compliance
|Network role |Interoperable international exchange |Domestic government reporting platform
|Core object |PINT invoice / UBL invoice |Government Uniform Invoice record
|Scope |B2B exchange and interoperability |Tax reporting, archival, and domestic compliance
|===

== TW-PINT as the interoperability bridge

[source,text]
----
Seller ERP
   ↓
TW-PINT Invoice
   ↓
Peppol Access Point
   ↓
Foreign or domestic trading partner

Optional local path:
TW-PINT Invoice
   ↓
eGUI Gateway
   ↓
Taiwan eGUI / tax reporting process
----

== What TW-PINT does not replace

TW-PINT does not replace Taiwan eGUI legal reporting obligations, MOF platform processes, domestic consumer eGUI functions, carrier/donation/lottery functions, or legal tax determination.
