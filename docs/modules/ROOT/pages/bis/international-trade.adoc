= International Trade Context
:toc: macro
:toclevels: 3

toc::[]

== Taiwan exporter to foreign buyer

A Taiwan exporter issues a TW-PINT invoice. The foreign buyer receives it through the Peppol network and processes it according to its local PINT environment.

== Foreign seller to Taiwan buyer

A Taiwan buyer may receive a PINT-compatible invoice from a foreign seller. The Taiwan buyer maps received invoice data into Taiwan accounting and tax reporting requirements.

== Cross-border zero-rated transactions

TW-PINT should support zero-rated export tax treatment when applicable.

== Foreign currency invoices

Foreign currency invoices should identify invoice currency and may require tax currency representation for Taiwan reporting alignment.

== Interoperability with JP-PINT and SG-PINT

TW-PINT should avoid unnecessary divergence from PINT to preserve interoperability with JP-PINT, SG-PINT, and other PINT specializations.
