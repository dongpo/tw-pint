:toc: macro
:toclevels: 3

toc::[]

== 4.1. Calculations

TW-PINT inherits PINT calculation principles and adds Taiwan-aligned tax calculation guidance.

== 4.2. Rounding

Rounding should follow PINT shared rules unless Taiwan reporting alignment requires more specific guidance.

== 4.3. Taiwan aligned calculations

Taiwan-aligned calculations include Business Tax calculation, tax subtotal alignment, foreign currency and tax-currency handling, and export invoice totals.

== 4.4. Taiwan-specific business rules

Taiwan-specific rules cover domestic seller and buyer identification, conditional handling for foreign parties, eGUI bridge data, credit notes, invoice number, tax category, and tax totals.
