= Annex B. Relationship of TW-PINT to Taiwan eGUI

== Conceptual mapping

[source,text]
----
Peppol / TW-PINT
        ↓
eGUI Gateway
        ↓
Taiwan eGUI / tax reporting
----

== Data that can be exchanged directly in TW-PINT

Seller and buyer identifiers, invoice number, invoice date, invoice line details, tax category, tax amount, monetary totals and supporting references.

== Data that may require downstream eGUI bridge processing

eGUI platform-specific status, consumer-facing carrier data, donation and lottery-related data, and local archival workflow data.

== Known gaps

Further analysis is required to produce an authoritative mapping between TW-PINT and Taiwan eGUI MIG.
