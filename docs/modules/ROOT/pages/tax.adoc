= Taiwan Business Tax

TW-PINT v0.1 supports three mainstream Taiwan Business Tax categories:

[cols="1,2,1", options="header"]
|===
|Code |Description |Rate
|S |Taxable |5%
|Z |Zero Rated |0%
|E |Exempt |0%
|===

Mixed tax is not represented as a separate tax category in v0.1. It should be expressed through multiple tax subtotals when applicable.
